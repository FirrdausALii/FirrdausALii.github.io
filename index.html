About Us

Magnificent as its name, Jenderam Hills Resort is located on a hill in Jenderam, Dengkil of Sepang District. Located just 20 minutes from KLIA, Jenderam Hills Resort covers an area of approximately 1.167 acres is quiet and equipped with a multi-purpose hall (Casanor Villa) and 2 luxurious villas that have a total of 10 rooms and 4 single bedroom with Modern Scandinavian design.

The specialty of Jenderam Hills Resort is its presence in a strategic rural area full of tranquility that will provide privacy and a cool and comfortable atmosphere resulting in the names of Villa Kamil and Villa Juliana.
